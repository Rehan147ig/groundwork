The Exposure Vector

Retrieval is becoming the new leak path.

Enterprise AI is being connected to internal knowledge faster than access controls are being rechecked. Without a runtime gate, sensitive context can move from private systems into an AI prompt before security teams have proof of what happened.

[01] Permission drift

Source access changes, but AI retrieval continues using stale assumptions.

[02] Unauthorized context

A relevant chunk can still be unsafe if the requester is not entitled to see it.

[03] Missing proof

Teams need evidence of what was allowed, blocked, and why.
