# WHISTLEBLOWER FOLLOW-UP — STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL

**SYNTHETIC — DEMO USE ONLY — NOT REAL BANK DATA**

**Ref:** AUDIT-2026-WHISTLEBLOWER
**Date:** 10 March 2026
**Owner:** Chair, Audit Committee of the Board
**Distribution:** Audit Committee members and the Chief Audit Executive only.

This document is the follow-up to a whistleblower report submitted via the bank's confidential channel in October 2025. Its existence and content are restricted to the Audit Committee. The Chief Audit Executive holds the underlying report and supporting workpapers separately.

## Summary

The follow-up review concludes that one of the three allegations in the original report is substantiated in part. The substantiated element concerns a procedural breach in a single transaction in 2024 and does not implicate any individual currently in a senior role. Two of the three allegations are unsubstantiated on the available evidence. Detailed findings are recorded in the underlying workpapers, which remain under restricted access at the discretion of the Chief Audit Executive.

## Status

The substantiated element has been remediated. No regulatory disclosure obligation is engaged. The Audit Committee has confirmed that the investigation can be closed.

—Chair, Audit Committee
