## Recommendation

Renew the existing £5,000,000 committed working capital facility for a further 12 months on substantially unchanged terms. Margin to be repriced from SONIA + 215bps to SONIA + 240bps reflecting current market spreads for the rating band. All other covenants and security to remain in place.

## Borrower background

Acme Manufacturing Ltd is a privately-held mid-market manufacturer of precision components for the European automotive supply chain. Customer of the bank since 2014. FY25 (audited) revenue £74.2M, EBITDA £8.9M, net debt £14.1M. Senior management unchanged. Cash conversion has been stable through FY25 despite softness in German OEM volumes; management has executed planned inventory rundown ahead of schedule.

## Risk commentary

Key risk remains end-market cyclicality. Stress scenario (German OEM volumes -15% sustained over 18 months) maintains covenant headroom on net leverage but tightens interest cover toward 3.2x. Management has shown willingness to flex capex in prior down-cycles. ESG review (carbon transition for ICE supply chain) is the principal medium-term concern — addressed in separate strategy note CM-STRAT-2026-003.
