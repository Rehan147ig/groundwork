**Risk Rating:** 5 (Acceptable / Watch)

## Recommendation

Approve a $2,000,000 secured equipment loan to GlobalTech Solutions Inc. for the purchase of two precision metrology systems. 60-month term, equal monthly amortization, pricing SOFR + 325bps. Security: first lien on the equipment financed plus an all-asset blanket security agreement.

## Borrower background

GlobalTech Solutions is a mid-market technology services company based in New York. Customer since 2019. FY25 (reviewed) revenue $42M, EBITDA $5.1M, net debt $11.8M. Trading is stable but ratio metrics have softened over the last two reporting periods due to a one-off contract delay with a major customer (now resolved per management).

## Risk commentary

GlobalTech sits one notch below comparable peers due to (i) elevated leverage post-FY24 expansion, (ii) high customer concentration (top three customers represent 64% of revenue). Equipment loan is well-collateralized — financed equipment has an active secondary market and projected book value comfortably covers the loan throughout the term.

The relationship is on credit watch following the FY25 covenant breach (since waived) on the existing revolver. RM recommends approval subject to (a) standing instruction that any further requests be escalated to Senior Credit; (b) quarterly management accounts requirement maintained; (c) review of customer concentration mitigation plan due 30 April 2026.
