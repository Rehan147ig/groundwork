## Recommendation

Approve participation as Joint Bookrunner and a $25,000,000 bilateral commitment in a $100,000,000 senior secured syndicated revolving credit facility for Wayne Enterprises Inc. Facility tenor 5 years, pricing SOFR + 175bps, undrawn fee 35bps. The bank's commitment is at the high end of historic levels for this client and is intended to consolidate our position as one of the top three relationship banks ahead of the anticipated 2027 capital markets activity.

## Strategic context

Wayne Enterprises represents the bank's single largest corporate relationship by total fee income over the trailing 24 months. The relationship is currently under competitive pressure from a recent overture by [Competitor Bank]. Increasing our commitment in this facility (from $15M historic to $25M proposed) is the operational expression of the Executive Committee's January 2026 decision to defend tier-1 corporate relationships against incumbent challengers.

## Bank-relationship economics

Estimated cross-sell economics for the relationship over the next 36 months: $9.2M fee income (M&A advisory, debt capital markets, cash management, FX). The incremental $10M commitment carries an expected capital cost of $2.8M over 5 years; the relationship NPV remains comfortably positive.

## Risk commentary

Borrower risk is unchanged from previous reviews — Wayne Enterprises is one of the strongest credits in the bank's corporate book. The strategic concentration risk (increasing our exposure to a single name to defend a relationship) has been escalated to and accepted by the Executive Risk Committee.
